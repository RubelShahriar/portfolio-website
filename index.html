How It Works

1. Submit Your Design

Send me your Figma or PSD source files. Ensure your design is well-organized with clear layers, groups, and naming conventions. You can also include any specific instructions or requirements to help me understand your vision better.

2. Initial Review and Quotation

As an experienced web designer I will review your submitted design. I'll assess the complexity and provide you with a detailed quotation and estimated timeline. During this step, I'll also clarify any ambiguities and confirm the project scope with you.

3. Development and Testing

Once you approve the quotation, as a developer will start converting your design into high-quality, responsive HTML/CSS code. I ensure that your design looks perfect across all devices and browsers. Throughout this process, I perform rigorous testing to guarantee functionality and compatibility.

4. Delivery and Revisions

After development, I'll deliver the HTML files to you for review. You can request any necessary revisions to ensure the final output meets your expectations. Once all adjustments are made, I'll provide the finalized files, ready for integration into your website or application.

FAQ

What services do you offer? +

We offer a comprehensive range of web design services including custom website design, responsive web design, e-commerce site design, website redesign, maintenance, and search engine optimization (SEO) integration.

Do you provide responsive design? +

Yes, we ensure that all HTML conversions are fully responsive, meaning they will work seamlessly across various devices and screen sizes, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Will my website be SEO-friendly? +

Yes, we design websites with SEO best practices in mind to help your site rank well in search engine results. This includes optimizing site structure, metadata, and ensuring fast load times.

What is the process for a Figma/PSD to HTML conversion project? +

Our process typically involves: 1. Initial consultation and requirement gathering. 2. Review and analysis of the design files. 3. Providing a quote and timeline. 4. Converting the design to HTML/CSS. 5. Quality assurance and testing. 6. Delivery of the final files.

How long does it take to convert a Figma/PSD design to HTML? +

The turnaround time depends on the complexity and size of the project. Typically, it can take anywhere from a few days to a couple of weeks. We will provide an estimated timeline after reviewing your design files.

What information do you need to start the conversion process? +

We need your design files (Figma or PSD), any specific instructions or requirements, and any additional assets (like images or fonts) that are part of the design.

How do you handle revisions and feedback? +

We include a certain number of revision rounds in our service. After delivering the initial conversion, you can review and provide feedback. We will make necessary adjustments based on your input.

What if I need help after the website is launched? +

We offer post-launch support to address any issues, make updates, or answer any questions you might have. You can choose from various support packages that best suit your needs.
